BLE Environmental Monitor

Project Overview

A compact, battery-powered environmental monitoring device that measures temperature, humidity, air pressure, and air quality. Features Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity for data transmission to mobile devices and cloud platforms, with months of battery life and weatherproof design for indoor and outdoor use.

Hardware Specifications

  • nRF52840 SoC (ARM Cortex-M4)
  • BME680 Environmental Sensor
  • MiCS-6814 Gas Sensor
  • E-Paper Display (2.9")
  • 3.7V LiPo Battery (2000mAh)
  • Solar Panel (5V, 1W)
  • Custom PCB (4-layer)
  • Weatherproof Enclosure (IP65)
  • Low-Power Crystal Oscillator
  • Charging Circuit with Protection
  • External Antenna for BLE
  • Magnetic Mount System

Firmware Architecture

Zephyr RTOS Implementation

Real-time operating system for efficient task scheduling and power management with dedicated threads for sensor reading, BLE communication, and display updates.

BLE Stack & Protocols

Custom GATT services for sensor data transmission with automatic reconnection and data buffering during connection loss.

Power Optimization

Advanced sleep modes, sensor polling optimization, and dynamic frequency scaling to achieve 6+ months battery life.
